title = "Customers Right to Cancel, and Keep Value"
date = 2025-03-27
+++
Because value is delivered in working, tested increments, customers may stop at any time and still retain a useful system that reflects the investment to date.

This is only possible if:
* Every iteration ends with integrated, shippable software.
* Documentation, telemetry, and operational handoffs keep the system usable without the team.
