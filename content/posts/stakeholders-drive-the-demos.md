+++
title = "Stakeholders Drive the Demos"
date = 2025-05-09
+++
Stakeholders operate the system during the demo. When stakeholders click the buttons and run the flows themselves, the team sees real reactions and honest feedback.

Stakeholder-driven demos remove the temptation that builder team might hide flaky features, and the team might discover gaps in the acceptance criteria, or might surface usability issues.

This hands-on approach builds trust, teaches stakeholders the system, and gives the team clearer, actionable data to fix what matters.

__Discussions for your team__
* Who will operate the demo next time?
* How will do we capture the stakeholders observations?
* What's our plan if something breaks during the demo?
