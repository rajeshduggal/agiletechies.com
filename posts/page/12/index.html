Agile Is a Small Idea

Robert C. Martin says that Agile is a small idea about the small problem of small programming teams doing small things. Agile is not a big idea about the big problem of big programming teams doing big things. Big things don’t get done by big teams; big things get done by the collaboration of many small teams doing many small things.
